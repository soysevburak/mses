Since the examination process should be honest and equal for every participant, the institution should ensure this equality. To maintain security and equality, most institutions choose human proctoring via a webcam, but that approach is not efficient.

We introduce a solution to this problem with an AI-powered online proctoring system. With MSES, we want to achieve more secure, just and efficient proctoring while recording exam takers video during examination, blocking other programs and screens, logging keyboard and mouse activities and process this data on cloud. Also MSES provides an easy-to-use examination environment with custom configurations to teachers, establishments and institutions.
